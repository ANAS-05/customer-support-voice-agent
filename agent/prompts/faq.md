# QuickBite FAQ

## Orders
- **How do I place an order?** Open the app, choose a restaurant, add items to your cart, then proceed to checkout and confirm your delivery address and payment method.
- **Can I schedule an order in advance?** Yes. At checkout, select "Schedule for later" and pick a date and time.
- **How do I cancel an order?** Go to Orders, select the active order, and tap "Cancel order." Free cancellation is available before the restaurant starts preparing your food.

## Delivery
- **How long does delivery take?** Typical delivery times are 25–45 minutes, depending on the restaurant, distance, and demand.
- **Can I track my driver?** Yes. Open the active order to see real-time driver location and ETA on the map.
- **How do I contact my driver?** Use the "Call" or "Message" button on the active order screen.

## Payments
- **What payment methods are accepted?** Major credit and debit cards, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and QuickBite gift cards.
- **Can I pay with cash?** Cash on delivery is available in select regions only.
- **Where do I find my receipts?** Receipts are available under Account > Order History for every completed order.

## Refunds & Issues
- **My order is late.** If your order is more than 30 minutes late with no update, contact support and we will investigate and compensate as appropriate.
- **An item is missing or wrong.** Report it within 48 hours of delivery for a redelivery or full refund, whichever you prefer.
- **How long do refunds take?** Refunds typically appear on your original payment method within 3–5 business days.

## Account
- **How do I change my address?** Go to Account > Addresses to add, edit, or delete saved delivery addresses.
- **How do I update my payment method?** Go to Account > Payment Methods to add or remove cards and digital wallets.
- **How do I delete my account?** Go to Account > Settings > Delete Account. Deletion is permanent and removes your order history.

## Promotions & Loyalty
- **Where do I enter a promo code?** Enter the code at checkout in the "Promo code" field before confirming your order.
- **What is QuickBite Rewards?** Our loyalty program lets you earn points on every order, redeemable for discounts and free items.

## Restaurants & Menus
- **Can I request special instructions?** Yes. Add a note for the restaurant at checkout (e.g., "no onions," "extra sauce").
- **Are menu prices the same as in-store?** Most restaurants match in-store prices, but some may vary slightly.
